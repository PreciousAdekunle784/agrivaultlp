The Crisis

African farmers grow the food and regenerate the soil — but capture almost none of the value.

₦4.3 Trillion

Lost to post-harvest waste annually in Nigeria. That's 40% of harvested food destroyed before reaching market.

<2%

Of global carbon credit revenue reaches African farmers — despite massive sequestration potential across the continent.

Invisible Spoilage

Zero visibility into when stored produce will degrade. By the time farmers notice, 30-40% is already gone.

Locked Out of Carbon

MRV costs $30-50 per soil sample. Smallholders can't afford the entry ticket to billion-dollar carbon markets.

No Buyer When Needed

No system to find a buyer fast when produce is about to spoil. Farmers lose everything or sell at rock-bottom prices.
